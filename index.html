Welcome

For as long as I can remember, I have been fascinated by technology. While it was fascinating, it was also mystifying. So I decided to learn how to code. Despite graduating with an economics degree and working as an online store manager, I decided to study computer science. Starting the month after graduating college, I began reading textbooks and solving exercises. I started with a couple of introductory computer science textbooks and then moved on to an algorithms textbook. After that, I learned web development and built a variety of projects. A couple of them are a markdown previewer and a timer.
